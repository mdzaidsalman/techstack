Elon Musk's response on Starlink arrival in India.
According to the SpaceX founder Elon Musk Starlink Satellite Internet may come to india very soon, It uses a group of satellites to serve Internet connectivity with lower latency. In february 2018 SpaceX has already launched their first two satellites. Currently they are serving only in 14 geo location which includes United States, United Kingdom, Australia, and Chile and so on. Starlink service is currently in beta testing like we got Jio without any download limit. Talking about speed, it offers from 50Mbps to 150Mbps and the latency is between 20ms to 40ms.
